# ⚔️ Perle Daily Battle

Perle Daily Battle is a live annotation challenge platform built for the [Perle Labs](https://perle.xyz) contributor community.

Every day, a new AI annotation task drops — pulled from one of Perle's real domains: Healthcare, Legal, Robotics, Finance, STEM, Linguistics, Philosophy, Psychology, Ethics, and Creative Writing. Contributors sign up, pick the better of two AI responses, write a justification, and get scored across four dimensions: correctness, depth, domain expertise, and clarity.

The best annotations rise to the top of a live leaderboard. Contributors can challenge each other to head-to-head battles by sharing a link — the opponent takes the same task on their own time, and scores are compared publicly.

---

## Why it exists

Perle Labs is building the human layer of AI — a network of contributors who provide high-quality feedback to make AI models smarter. This app puts that mission into a format people actually want to engage with: a daily challenge that sharpens your annotation skills, rewards the best thinkers, and makes the whole thing competitive and social.

It was built as part of the Perle Labs community campaign to show what serious contributors look like in practice — not a landing page, not a mockup, but a fully working product anyone in the community can use.
